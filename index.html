What We Do

Comprehensive Floor Care Solutions

From initial strip and wax to routine maintenance programs, we handle every stage of your floor's lifecycle at healthcare-grade quality.

Floor Stripping & Waxing

Complete removal of old finish buildup followed by professional-grade wax application for a clean, protected surface.

High-Speed Buffing & Burnishing

Restore shine and reduce surface scratches with high-RPM burnishing using commercial-grade floor burnishers.

Deep Scrubbing

Rotary machine deep-cleaning to remove embedded dirt and grime from vinyl, linoleum, tile, and composite surfaces.

Routine Maintenance Programs

Scheduled service plans designed to maintain floor appearance and reduce long-term wear and replacement costs.
