Core Competencies

Drewry Floor Care Services provides healthcare-grade hard-floor maintenance services to commercial, institutional, and government facilities. All services meet or exceed the cleanliness and documentation standards required in healthcare and federal settings.

✓ Floor Stripping & Waxing — Complete removal of old finish and professional multi-coat wax application on VCT, linoleum, vinyl composite, and resilient surfaces.
✓ High-Speed Burnishing — 1,500–2,000+ RPM commercial burnishing for gloss restoration, finish hardening, and slip-resistance maintenance.
✓ Rotary Machine Scrubbing — Deep extraction of embedded soils using surface-matched pad selection; wet vacuum residue removal.
✓ Scheduled Maintenance Programs — Customized recurring service contracts with documented service logs and compliance reporting.
✓ Healthcare & Institutional Floor Care — Services scaled for medical facilities, VA buildings, schools, and government offices requiring infection-control compliance.
✓ Federal Facility Maintenance — Floor care for federal buildings and VA facilities under VOSB set-aside programs with full documentation.
✓ Low-VOC Product Usage — All finishes and cleaning products meet LEED and EPA standards for occupied healthcare and institutional spaces.
✓ OSHA Safety Compliance — Wet floor signage, PPE usage, and documented hazard communication protocols on every service call.
