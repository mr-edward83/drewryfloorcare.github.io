Core Services

Everything Your Floors Need — Under One Contract

Service 01

Floor Stripping & Waxing

Over time, floor finish builds up unevenly, trapping dirt and creating a dull, yellowed appearance. Our strip-and-wax service removes all layers of old finish down to the bare floor surface, then applies fresh, professional-grade wax in multiple coats — achieving the kind of clean, uniform surface required in healthcare and institutional settings.

  • Full removal of old finish using industrial-strength stripping solution
  • Rotary machine agitation for thorough product penetration
  • Wet vacuum extraction of stripper residue — no residue left behind
  • Multiple coats of professional, low-VOC floor finish applied
  • Appropriate for VCT, linoleum, vinyl composite, and other resilient floors
  • Wet floor sign placement and full OSHA safety compliance
♿
Strip & Wax
Service 02

High-Speed Buffing & Burnishing

Between full strip-and-wax cycles, buffing and burnishing keep your floors looking freshly finished. Our commercial-grade burnishers operate at 1,500–2,000+ RPM to redistribute and harden existing floor finish — restoring a high-gloss shine without the cost or downtime of a full strip. This is especially effective in healthcare facilities where appearance and slip-resistance must be maintained continuously.

  • Commercial-grade high-speed floor burnisher
  • Restores gloss and reduces surface scuffs and micro-scratches
  • Ideal for monthly or quarterly maintenance cycles
  • Significantly extends life of existing floor finish
  • Low disruption — performed during off-hours or low-traffic periods
🛣
Buffing & Burnishing
Service 03

Deep Scrubbing

High-traffic floors accumulate grime, scuff marks, and embedded soils that standard mopping cannot remove. Our rotary machine deep-scrubbing service uses aggressive pads and appropriate cleaning solutions to extract deeply embedded dirt — the kind of thorough cleaning that infection-control standards in healthcare and government settings require.

  • Rotary floor machine with surface-matched pad selection
  • Effective on VCT, linoleum, ceramic tile, quarry tile, and composites
  • Grout line scrubbing available for tiled surfaces
  • Wet vacuum extraction for clean, dry, residue-free results
  • Can be combined with a recoat service for immediate finish restoration
💤
Deep Scrubbing
Service 04

Scheduled Maintenance Programs

Reactive floor care is more expensive and disruptive than proactive maintenance. Our customized programs are designed to keep floors consistently clean and protected throughout the year — extending finish life, reducing replacement cycles, and ensuring compliance with facility appearance and hygiene standards at all times.

  • Custom schedules: weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or quarterly
  • Tiered program options based on traffic volume and floor type
  • Service logs and maintenance records provided for compliance
  • Priority scheduling for contract clients
  • Annual strip-and-wax included in full-program contracts
  • Ideal for schools, medical facilities, offices, and government buildings
