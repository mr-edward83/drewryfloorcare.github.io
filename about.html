About Drewry Floor Care Services

A veteran-owned family business delivering healthcare-grade floor care built on military discipline, accountability, and a relentless commitment to excellence.

Our Story

From Uniform to Business — A Mission That Continues

Drewry Floor Care Services, LLC was founded by Michael Drewry, a U.S. Army Staff Sergeant who has spent years leading teams, maintaining high standards, and executing missions with precision. That same ethos drives every aspect of this company.

Joined by his wife Joann, who brings direct expertise in Environmental & Floor Care Services management, and son Dakota, who serves as Operations Manager, Drewry Floor Care is a true family enterprise — rooted in trust, built for performance.

Our goal is to be fully operational and serving clients by March–April 2027, establishing ourselves as the premier healthcare-grade floor care provider for commercial and government facilities across Arkansas and the surrounding region.

Our Promise: Every floor we service reflects the same standard of quality we would demand of ourselves. No shortcuts, no excuses — just results at healthcare-grade standards.
