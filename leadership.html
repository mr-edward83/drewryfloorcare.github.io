MD

Michael Drewry

Managing Member & Director of Operations

Michael Drewry is the founder and Managing Member & Director of Operations of Drewry Floor Care Services, LLC, bringing a career of military leadership and service to the company's foundation. As a U.S. Army Staff Sergeant (SSG), Michael has spent years developing the skills that define great business leadership: planning and executing complex operations, managing personnel under pressure, maintaining standards in demanding environments, and driving results with limited resources.

As the majority owner and managing member, Michael holds 51%+ ownership and full operational control — meeting the criteria for Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) certification. His military background informs every aspect of the company's culture: punctuality, accountability, standardized procedures, and zero tolerance for substandard work.

Michael is committed to building Drewry Floor Care into a sustainable, growth-oriented business that serves both private commercial clients and federal agencies with healthcare-grade standards across Arkansas.

JD

Joann Drewry

Co-Owner & Director of Environmental & Floor Care Services

Joann Drewry is a co-owner of Drewry Floor Care Services and serves as the company's Director of Environmental & Floor Care Services. Joann brings direct, hands-on expertise in the environmental and floor care services field — the professional discipline that encompasses janitorial, custodial, and floor maintenance operations in institutional and commercial settings.

Her background in environmental and floor care services gives the company a critical operational edge: she understands the standards, procedures, and regulatory requirements of professional floor care from the inside — including the infection-control and cleanliness benchmarks that define healthcare-grade service delivery. Joann oversees quality assurance, service delivery standards, and standard operating procedures.

As a co-owner invested personally and financially in the company's success, Joann brings both the technical knowledge and the leadership commitment that drives consistent, healthcare-grade results for every client.

Dakota Drewry

Operations Manager & General Partner

Dakota Drewry serves as Drewry Floor Care Services' Operations Manager, responsible for day-to-day coordination of service delivery, scheduling, equipment logistics, and field team oversight. Dakota holds a General Partner (GP) stake in the business and is actively involved in the company's growth and operational development.

In his role, Dakota acts as the operational backbone of the company — translating strategic direction from the leadership team into executed service calls, ensuring that crews are prepared and equipped, that client sites are ready, and that service documentation is properly maintained for compliance and audit purposes.

Dakota's investment in both the company's financial success and day-to-day operations makes him an essential part of the Drewry family enterprise — a leadership team that works together with shared purpose and mutual accountability.
